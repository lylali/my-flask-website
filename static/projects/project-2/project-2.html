In the realm of healthcare, the communication between hospitals and General Practitioners (GPs) plays a pivotal role in ensuring seamless patient care post-hospitalization. The existing challenges of varied structures and insufficient information in discharge summaries often lead to post-handover doctors grappling with unfamiliar patient cases. Recognizing these issues, our project proposes a comprehensive solution leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) to streamline the process and enhance the quality of discharge summaries.

Main Problems Addressed:

1. Lack of Standardized Structure: Current discharge summaries lack a standardized structure, leading to inconsistencies and crucial information being overlooked.

2. Insufficient Information for GPs: Post-handover doctors often find themselves in the challenging position of deciphering incomplete or unclear information when taking over patient care.

Proposed Solution:

Our innovative solution revolves around an AI-powered summarization system integrated into a user-friendly web application. The key features include:

1. Automated Summarization: By uploading relevant health notes, our AI swiftly generates a comprehensive discharge summary letter, ensuring standardization and completeness.

2. Doctor Editing:The generated summary is editable by the doctor, providing flexibility and allowing customization based on their knowledge and patient specifics.

3. Patient-Friendly Explanation:A concise patient-friendly paragraph is appended to the summary, enhancing communication between healthcare professionals and patients.

4. AI Recommendations:The system provides insightful AI-generated recommendations for any crucial information missed by the doctor, empowering them to make informed decisions.

Revenue Models:

Our sustainable revenue models encompass:

1. Licensing: Hospitals and healthcare institutions can acquire licenses for system integration, ensuring seamless adoption across their networks.

1. Subscription: Offering subscription plans for individual doctors or medical practices, providing continuous access to the AI-powered summarization tool.

1. Feature-based Selling:Individual features, such as AI recommendations or patient-friendly explanations, can be sold separately to cater to diverse needs.

Differentiators:

What sets our solution apart is not just automation but the incorporation of AI recommendations, addressing any potential oversights by doctors. This not only speeds up routine tasks but also ensures accuracy, reducing the risk of manual data entry errors in discharge summaries.

Impact and Future Prospects:

Our project not only addresses the immediate challenges but also contributes to relieving the burden on junior doctors, allowing them more time for direct patient care and professional development. Moreover, the incorporation of an AI chatbot for tailored patient care suggestions adds an extra layer of efficiency and reduces cognitive load, further benefiting GPs in managing follow-ups and providing comprehensive care.

By standardizing and enhancing the discharge summary process, our project aims to significantly improve communication, minimize errors, and ultimately contribute to a more efficient and patient-centric healthcare system.
